# 2026-04-17 — Session 02 — First real experiment

**Tried a fast-draft approach.** Wanted to see if we could skip the structured-model step and go straight from prompt to output. Recorded as `experiments/2026-04-17-example-fast-draft.md`.

**Result: it worked for trivial cases and failed for any non-trivial branching.** Confirmed that the intermediate-model step is load-bearing, not ceremony. This is the kind of negative result that is worth preserving — it saves the team re-trying the same shortcut in three months.
